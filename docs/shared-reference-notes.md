# Shared ChatGPT Reference Notes

Source: https://chatgpt.com/share/6a4682ab-a9c8-83e8-bc23-1c5b6545e6ea

## Brand

- Product name: VolEdge AI
- Primary tagline in master spec: Where Volatility Becomes Opportunity
- Alternate tagline from the reference: Find the Edge. Trade with Confidence.
- Product position: an AI-powered ETF intelligence platform, not a generic stock screener or charting platform.

## Home Screen Metrics

The reference says the home screen should revolve around four core metrics:

- VolEdge Score
- Historical Accuracy
- Confidence Level
- Risk/Reward

The VolEdge Score must remain the largest visual element. Historical Accuracy should be the second most prominent metric.

## Product Architecture

- Opportunity Radar
- Sector Volatility Radar
- Pattern Recognition AI
- Historical Accuracy Engine
- ETF Intelligence
- Gold & Silver Intelligence
- Volatility Intelligence
- Portfolio Intelligence
- Alert Center

## UI Direction

The shared page includes generated dark fintech dashboard concepts described as:

- Fintech trading dashboard overview
- Dark fintech dashboard with AI alerts
- AI-powered ETF trading dashboard mockup
- AI-driven ETF analysis dashboard interface
- Sleek AI-powered ETF trading dashboard

Design interpretation for implementation:

- Premium dark fintech interface.
- Inspired by Robinhood, TradingView, and Coinbase without copying them.
- Use leaderboard cards instead of spreadsheet-style tables.
- Keep the dashboard uncluttered.
- Reveal deeper technical detail after a user selects an opportunity.
- Make alerts feel operational and decision-support focused.

## Safety

Version 1 remains alert-only. Every recommendation must require manual approval and include suggested entry, stop loss, target, risk/reward, historical accuracy, and an AI explanation.
